Luscious Layers: The Allure of Lasagna"

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has captured the hearts and taste buds of people around the world. This mouthwatering dish consists of layers of wide, flat pasta noodles, rich and savory meat or vegetarian sauces, creamy béchamel sauce, and generous amounts of cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan.
